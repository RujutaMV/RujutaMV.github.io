Vishwa Dharma Marathon - 2023

This is an yearly event that is conducted by HSS at the European Main Land level (EML) from the shareerik team including all the EML countries only.
The aim of this event is to complete kilometers equal to one rotation of the Earth(40,075km). Along with it, we would have better health and healthy competition. The activities included are: Running, Cycling, Walking & Swimming. The kms can be tracked by using apps of personal choice like strava, google fit etc. or digital watches.
